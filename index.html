// 01 — ABOUT

Who I Am

A self-taught developer passionate about making tech accessible to everyone.

I'm Shadrack, the developer and educator behind Tech Amera. My mission is simple: lower the barrier to entry for anyone who wants to learn to code — especially in Africa where resources can be hard to come by.

I started this platform as a small demo and it grew into a full resource hub. I believe programming is one of the most powerful skills a person can learn, and I'm committed to sharing everything I know along the way.

When I'm not coding, I'm curating learning resources, creating content, or collaborating with the community on social media.
